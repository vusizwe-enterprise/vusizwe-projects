Our Services

VIP Protection

Our discreet and professional VIP protection team ensures the safety of high-profile individuals, including celebrities, executives, diplomats, and public figures. Our agents are trained in threat assessment, evasive driving, and secure transportation.

Armed & Unarmed Guards

We offer highly trained security guards—both armed and unarmed—tailored to your property's security needs. Our guards undergo regular refresher training and adhere to strict professionalism and code of conduct.

Retail Security Officers

Our retail guards are customer-service oriented while remaining vigilant to prevent theft, monitor CCTV systems, and provide a safe shopping environment for staff and customers.

Special Events Security

From concerts to corporate functions, our event security team manages crowd control, access points, and emergency response to ensure a smooth and secure experience for all attendees.

Mining Division Protection

Our mining security specialists provide round-the-clock security at mines and high-risk industrial areas, ensuring compliance, access control, and asset protection even in remote locations.

Crowd Management & Control

Using a combination of strategic planning, personnel, and communication tools, we manage and de-escalate crowds effectively at events, protests, and busy venues to maintain safety and order.

Armed Response

Our 24/7 rapid response teams are trained to handle all alarm situations with speed and professionalism, using modern communication systems and armed backup when necessary.

Security & Firearm Training

We provide accredited training courses for individuals and security personnel, covering firearm handling, self-defense, and legal compliance under industry regulations.

SASSETA Learnership Programs

Our learnerships empower youth with practical skills and security qualifications certified by SASSETA, opening pathways to employment and personal growth.
