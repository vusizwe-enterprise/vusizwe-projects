Training Programs

At Vusizwe, we believe in empowering individuals with practical security skills and certified training that meet industry standards. Our training division is led by experienced professionals and accredited by relevant authorities.

Security Officer Grades (Grade A – E)

We offer PSIRA-registered security officer training for all levels. These courses prepare individuals for various roles including site supervisor, control room operations, and basic guarding functions.

Firearm Training

Our accredited firearm courses cover safe handling, maintenance, and use of firearms in high-pressure environments. This includes basic and business-purpose firearm training modules.

Armed Response Training

Designed for professionals in rapid response units, this course focuses on armed interventions, tactical driving, conflict resolution, and legal procedures under the Firearms Control Act.

SASSETA Learnership Programs

Our Security Learnership programs provide unemployed youth with NQF-aligned education, workplace experience, and nationally recognized qualifications in safety and security.

First Aid & Emergency Response

We offer first aid levels 1–3 and emergency medical response training to equip security personnel with life-saving skills during incidents or workplace injuries.

Specialized Courses

  • VIP Close Protection Training
  • Control Room Operations
  • Event & Crowd Management
  • Conflict Management & Communication
