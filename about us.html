About Us

Founded in Johannesburg, Vusizwe Business Enterprise & Projects is a proudly South African security company committed to delivering exceptional protection services. With a foundation rooted in integrity, discipline, and professionalism, we provide peace of mind to clients across various industries.

Our Mission

To protect life and property by offering dependable, high-quality, and customized security solutions through highly trained personnel and cutting-edge strategies.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and reliable security service provider in South Africa and beyond, ensuring safety in every environment we serve.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold the highest ethical standards.
  • Excellence: We are committed to superior performance.
  • Respect: We treat everyone with dignity and fairness.
  • Innovation: We embrace continuous improvement and new technologies.
  • Community: We empower local communities through job creation and training programs.
